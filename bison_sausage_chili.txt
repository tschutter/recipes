Bison and Sausage Chili

1 pound chopped bison
1 pound pork sausage
1 cup water

1/2 cup diced onion
1 diced green pepper
1/4 cup red chili powder
2 tablespoons cumin
1 tablespoon salt
1 tablespoon white pepper
1 tablespoon sugar
6 cloves chopped garlic
1 4 ounce can green chili strips

2 15 ounce cans kidney beans
2 15 ounce cans pinto beans
1 28 ounce can diced tomato
2 tablespoons vinegar
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
2 teaspoons beef bullion
1 teaspoon chicken bullion

Cook bison, sausage, and 1 cup water in stock pot until browned.
Drain.

Add onion, green pepper, red chili powder, cumin, salt, white pepper,
sugar, garlic, and green chili. Simmer 15 minutes.

Add kidney beans, pinto beans, tomato, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce,
beef bullion, and chicken bullion.  Bring to a boil.  May need to add
1 to 2 cups of water to bring to the desired consistency.

Simmer for an hour.
