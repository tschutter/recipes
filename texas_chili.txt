Texas Chili

3-1/2 pounds top round, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
5 tablespoons oil
2 cups chopped onions
4 cloves minced garlic
3 tablespoons chili powder
1-1/2 teaspoons oregano
3 teaspoons ground cumin (chili flavor)
1 teaspoon crushed red peppers (optional)
2 cups beef broth
1 28 ounce can diced tomatoes
1 6 ounce can tomato paste
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar
1 26 ounce can red kidney beans

Brown meat in oil.  Cook onion in meat, drain.  Add rest.  Simmer till
meat is tender - about 2 hours.

source: Lady Byrd Johnson
keywords: beef, stew
