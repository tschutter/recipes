Overnight High-Protein Oatmeal

1 cup steel-cut oats
2 large apples, cored and chopped
1.5 cups unsweetened non-dairy milk
3 cups water
2 teaspoons cinnamon
0.5 teaspoon salt
4 tablespoons hemp seeds
4 tablespoons chia seeds
4 tablespoons brown sugar
2 tablespoons coconut oil
2 tablespoons real maple syrup or organic or local honey

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Grease a slow cooker with coconut oil, butter, or an oil spray.

Place all ingredients except the vanilla in the slow cooker.

Cover, and cook on low 7-8 hours.

Add vanilla before serving.
