Wild Rice with Pork Sausage

1 cup wild rice

1 pound pork sausage

1 cup celery, diced
1 medium onion, diced

1-1/2 teaspoon salt
1-1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning (if not seasoned sausage)

1 can cream of mushroom soup

Prepare rice.  Brown sausage.  Drain excess grease from sausage.

Boil celery and onion in water.  Drain, saving one cup of this water.

Mix rice, sausage, celery, onion, salt, and poultry seasoning.  Place
in baking dish.  Add soup and 1 cup water saved from vegetables.

*NOTE* Original recipe author was a ditz.  There may or may not be an
additional cup of water required.

Bake 1-1/2 hours at 325 degrees F.  Stir and uncover last 15 minutes
of baking time.
