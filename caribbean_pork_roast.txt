Caribbean Pork Roast

3-4 sweet potatoes, cut in 1-inch cubes
2 onions, cut in 1/2-inch slices

4-5 lb. pork roast
1 tablespoon sugar
2 teaspoons coarse salt
2 teaspoons pepper
1 teaspoon ground coriander
1 teaspoon ground allspice

3/4 cup dark rum
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup orange juice

The night before, cutup onions and sweet potatoes and place on bottom
of crock pot.  Combine the sugar, salt, pepper, coriander, and
allspice.  Rub spice mixture over the pork roast.  Place roast in
crock pot.  Refrigerate overnight.

The next morning, combine the rum, honey, and orange juice.  Pour over
the roast in the pot.  Cook on low for 9-10 hours, or on high for 5-6
hours.
