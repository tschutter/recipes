Salsa Cruda
-----------

- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 white onion, chopped
- 3 green onions, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 jalapeno, diced
- garlic salt to taste
- salt to taste
- black pepper to taste

Mix all.
