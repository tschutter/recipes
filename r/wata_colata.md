Wata Colata
-----------

- 1 inch thick watermelon slice
- 3 tablespoons Simply Orange Mango
- 1/2 cup blueberries
- 6 tablespoons agave nectar or honey
- 7 frozen peaches (sliced)
- 3 medium bananas

Thoroughly mix in a blender.

Add ice before serving.
