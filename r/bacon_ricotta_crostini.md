Bacon Ricotta Crostini
----------------------

- 1 baguette
- 16 ounces bacon
- 8 ounces ricotta cheese
<!-- -->
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon hot sauce

Cut baguette at an angle and toast slices.

Top slices with ricotta and cooked bacon pieces.

Mix honey and hot sauce and drizzle over bacon.
