Salsa Ranchera
--------------

- 2 tomatoes
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 2 serrano chiles, minced
- juice of 1/2 lime
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- ? teaspoon salt

Submerge the tomatoes in boiling water for 8 seconds.  Peel and dice
tomatoes.

Mix in rest of ingredients.
