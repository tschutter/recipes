Unbaked Chocolate Cookies
-------------------------

- 6 tablespoons cocoa
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
<!-- -->
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 cups quick oats
- 1 cup chopped nuts

Mix cocoa, milk, sugar, and butter in saucepan.  Boil for 2 to 3
minutes.  Remove from heat.

Add vanilla, oats, and nuts.  Mix well and quickly.

Drop by teaspoonful onto waxed paper.
