Sugar Cookies
-------------

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Mix all.  Roll into balls the size of walnuts.  Dip top half into cold
water then sugar.
