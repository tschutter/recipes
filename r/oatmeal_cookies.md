Oatmeal Cookies
---------------

- 1 cup oil
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 2 cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 cups oatmeal
- 1 cup nuts

Mix all ingredients.  Drop by spoonfuls on a greased cookie sheet.

Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes.
