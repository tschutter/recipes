Chocolate-Mint Fizz
-------------------

- 2 tablespoons syrup
- 2 tablespoons whipped cream
- 2 tablespoons club soda
- 2 scoops peppermint ice cream

Stir together syrup, whipped cream, and club soda until foamy.  Add
ice cream.  Fill with club soda.
