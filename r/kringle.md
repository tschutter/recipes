Kringle (Kringla)
-----------------

- 1-1/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1/2 cup margarine
<!-- -->
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 3-1/2 cups flour

Cream sugar, egg, and margarine.  Mix in rest.  Chill at least one
hour.

Roll out with a little flour.  Form into pretzel shapes.

Bake at 500 degrees for 3 minutes.
