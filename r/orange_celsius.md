Orange Celsius (aka Orange Julius)
----------------------------------

- 1/2 cup orange juice
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 4 ice cubes

Add to blender and blend until smooth.  Makes 1 cup.
