Pico de Gallo
-------------

- 1/2 cup white onion, diced
- 1/2 cup tomato, diced
- 1/2 cup cilantro
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- salt
- pepper
- lime juice
- olive oil

Mix onion, tomato, cilantro, and sugar.  Season to taste with salt and
pepper.  Drizzle with lime juice and olive oil.  Mix before serving.
