Chocolate Spritz

4 squares unsweetened chocolate

1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
2 eggs

4 tablespoons milk
4 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt

Melt the chocolate in the microwave and set aside to cool.

Cream shortening.  Add sugar and cream thoroughly.  Add eggs unbeaten.

Alternately add milk, flour, and salt.

Mix in the cooled chocolate.

Fill a cookie press.  Use the camel disk or the bumpy strip disk.
Form cookies on ungreased cookie sheet.  Sprinkle with colored sugar.

Bake for 7 minutes at 400 degrees F.
